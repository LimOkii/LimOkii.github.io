Yifei Lu (卢逸飞)

Research on LLM Agents, Tool Learning, and Reinforcement Learning

I obtained my master's degree from Northeastern University under the supervision of Prof. Feiliang Ren. I am currently working at Qiantang Credit. Previously, I conducted research and engineering internships at Meituan(AI Search), Tencent(AI Lab & Hunyuan Digital Human), and DeepLang AI.

My research focuses on large language model agents, tool learning, and reinforcement learning. I am especially interested in building autonomous agents that can invoke tools, learn from interaction, and complete long-horizon tasks that require planning, reasoning, and reliable execution.
